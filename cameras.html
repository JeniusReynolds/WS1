Canon EOS 6D

20.2MP Full Frame Sensor | 1080p HD Video | ISO: 100 - 25600

The Canon EOS 6D camera body is an entry level Pro quality camera. It is currently priced on Amazon.com for $1,799.00. The camera sports 20.2 megapixel full frame sensor. The full frame is what makes it a Pro Level camera. The Canon EOS 6D is also a very capable video recorder with the ability to record videos in 1080p resolution which is still top of the line for cameras which have a primary focus on photo taking rather than video. With a full frame sensor the Canon EOS 6D can expand its native ISO settings to 102400. With an IOS setting that high low light photography becomes effortless because this allows the camera to focus quickly and sharply. The Canon EOS 6D is a great entry point camera for those looking to enter into the professional realm of photography.
