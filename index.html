This Weeks Featured Camera Equipment





Featured Camera

Nikon D600




The Nikon D600 camera body is an entry level Pro quality camera. It is currently priced on Amazon.comm for $1,357.49. The camera has an impressive 24.3 megapixel full fram sensor. The full frame sensor is what makes this a Pro Level camera. The Nikon D600 also shoots video in 1080p resolution which is still industry leading for top level cameras that have photography as their primary purpose. With a full frame sensor the Nikon D600 can expand its native ISO from 6400 to 25600. With an IOS setting that high it allows the camera to take low light photos efficiently and sharply. Although the Nikon D600 does not have as high an ISO setting as its Canon EOS 6D counterpart, it is still a very solid and efficient pro level camera for new professionals.

Featured Lens

Sigma 150-500mm




The Sigma 150-500mm is a mid to pro level zoom telephoto lens. It is currently priced on Amazon.com for $869.00. This is a high quality zoom lens for both Canon and Nikon camera bodies(They make an equivalent model for each). This lens is a great lens for portrait photography. This lens would not be the best choice for low light photography because of its rather slow 5 aperture. This lens is a solid addition to any photographer looking for a high quality telephoto lens.

Featured Flash

Nikon SB800




The Nikon SB800 Flash is a very solid performing off body flash. The Nikon SB800 is an automated flash that adjusts its settings based upon what the user has in focus on their Nikon camera body. It is currently being sold on amazon for $729.00. It is a pro level flash for photographers that are looking to shoot anything from portraits to landscapes and anything inbetween.
